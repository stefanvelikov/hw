FAQ's

Got Questions? We've Got Answers

How will a new website bring in more clients?

A modern, engaging website builds trust and credibility, making it easy for potential clients to find your salon and book an appointment.

Will my website reflect my salon's unique style and brand?

Yes, our design team works closely with you to ensure your website perfectly captures the style, mood, and branding of your salon.

What makes hairWeb different from other web services?

We specialize in the hair salon industry, using science-backed strategies to target your ideal clients and boost your business.

How long does a website refresh take?

Our typical project timeline is 4-6 weeks, depending on your needs and the level of customization required.

Can I update my new website myself?

Yes! Our sites are user-friendly, allowing you to easily update content, photos, and more as your business evolves.

Will my new website work on mobile devices?

Absolutely. We ensure your site looks stunning and functions smoothly on every device, from smartphones to desktops.

Will the website integrate with my booking system?

Yes, we can integrate your current booking system, or we can recommend the best options to streamline appointments directly through your website.
